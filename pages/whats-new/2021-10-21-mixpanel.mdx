Grouparoo has added support for exporting data to [Mixpanel](https://mixpanel.com/)!

With the `@grouparoo/mixpanel` plugin, you can now sync data from your data warehouse into Mixpanel's User Profiles, Properties and Groups.

Learn more about integrating Grouparoo with Mixpanel [here](/integrations/destinations/mixpanel).
