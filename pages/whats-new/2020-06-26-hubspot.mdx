You can now connect your Grouparoo profiles and groups to [Hubspot](https://www.hubspot.com). With this destination, you can empower your sales teams on the Hubspot platform with anything from your data warehouse.

Grouparoo will make contacts with properties and add the to lists inside the Hubspot environment.
