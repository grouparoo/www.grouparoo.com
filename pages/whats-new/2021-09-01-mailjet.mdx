With the release of Grouparoo 0.6.0, you can sync all of your customer data that lives
in your data warehouse into [Mailjet](https://www.mailjet.com/) with the `@grouparoo/mailjet` plugin.

For more information and use cases, check out you can use the
[Mailjet destination page](/integrations/destinations/mailjet).
