With the release of Grouparoo v0.6, you can sync all of your customer data that lives
in your data warehouse into [Mailjet](https://www.mailjet.com/) with the `@grouparoo/mailjet` plugin.

Learn more about integrating with Grouparoo with Mailjet [here](/integrations/destinations/mailjet).
