We're excited to share our latest release with a ton of new features, bug fixes, and updates:

**Batch Imports & Updated Dashboard**

We've revamped a bunch of how Grouparoo works under the hood, simplifying how our data imports work and also improving performance. As such, we've also given the Grouparoo dashboard a refresh to better explain the current state of how Grouparoo is managing your data.

Now you can choose between SQLite and Postgres for the database system underlying Grouparoo. You can make these modifications in your environment variables.
