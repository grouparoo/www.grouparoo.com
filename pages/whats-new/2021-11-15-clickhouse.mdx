Grouparoo 0.74 introduces the `@grouparoo/clickhouse` plugin to sync data from and to
[ClickHouse](https://clickhouse.com/) databases. Learn more about connecting to ClickHouse [in the plugin documentation](/docs/plugins/grouparoo-clickhouse).
