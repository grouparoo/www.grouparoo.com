With the release of Grouparoo 0.7.4 you can [sync data from and to
a ClickHouse database](/integrations/sources/clickhouse) with the `@grouparoo/clickhouse` plugin.

For more information and examples, check out the
[ClickHouse plugin docs](/docs/plugins/grouparoo-clickhouse).
