Grouparoo's [Postgres](https://www.postgresql.org/) plugin enables you to import data from one or more Postgres databases or to export data to a Postgres database.

This guide will show you how to work with the Postgres plugin to [create a Source](#create-postgres-source) to import your data, or to [create a Destination](#create-postgres-destination) to export your data.
