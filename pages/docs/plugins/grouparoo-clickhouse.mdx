Grouparoo's [ClickHouse](https://www.clickhouse.com/) Plugin enables you to import data from one or more ClickHouse databases or to export data to a ClickHouse database.

This guide will show you how to work with the ClickHouse Plugin to [create a Source](#create-clickhouse-source) to import your data, or to [create a Destination](#create-clickhouse-destination) to export your data.
